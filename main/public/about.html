About File Sharing Room

This website is maintained and developed by Jothi Prakasam Ramesh, a sophomore at Sairam Institute of Technology.

Welcome to File Sharing Room, a platform designed to facilitate easy and secure file sharing among group members. With our application, you can create rooms, set passwords for security, and share files seamlessly within the room. This ensures that only authorized users can access the shared files, making it perfect for collaborative projects, team communications, and secure document distribution.

Our goal is to provide a user-friendly interface that makes file sharing straightforward and efficient. We hope you find our service useful and we are constantly working to improve your experience. If you have any suggestions or feedback, please feel free to reach out to us.
